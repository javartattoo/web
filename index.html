Behind the Ink

Every artist has a DNA—mine is made of graphite, adrenaline, and pure grit.

Origin

Started with a stolen sketchbook and a hunger for the hustle. I cut my teeth in the underground scene, learning that every scar has a story and every line needs to be earned. This isn't just a career; it's a lifelong obsession with the craft.

Specialty

I don't play it safe. My lane is heavy blackwork and high-contrast realism—pieces that hit hard from across the street. If it's got grit, detail, and a bit of soul, I'm the one you want behind the machine.

Fuel

Fueled by 808 basslines, cold brew, and the relentless drive to outdo my last piece. I don't stop until the vision is locked in and the ink is perfect. The energy in the booth stays high so the art stays sharp"

Mission

To leave a permanent mark on the culture, one client at a time. I'm not here to just 'do a tattoo'—I'm here to build armor that you wear with pride. My goal is for you to walk out feeling more like yourself than when you walked in.
